Shishu vidya Mandir
Banshidhar,nagar,untari,jharkhand

SARASWATI SHISHU VIDYA MANDIR is a co-educational English medium school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi and is situated at Dhurwa of Heavy Engineering Corporation (HEC) township, Ranchi, serene and beautiful surrounding. The school aims at giving a qualitative high standard of education which would develop the students innate potential to the fullest. It is managed by Shishu Vikas Mandir Samiti (Jharkhand), Dhurwa, Ranchi, A registered body under the India Societies Registration Act of 1860 with eminent educationists and philanthropistsk
